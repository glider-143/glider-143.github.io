Promotional Movie

Two weeks after it was in the can, our promotional movie is now finally ready to go. Honestly, I am not quite satisfied with it. First, unlike the previous video which was edited by one of my classmates, this one did not require any creativity. I just put all the footages that I shot together, adding subtitles, audio tracks and a background music. It was boring and time consuming, you can see how many pieces there was in the project file. On the other hand, our last video was more like an advertisement with fancy transitions, upgrades, after effects and more aerials (in fact, the aerial scenes in this video were ‘borrowed’ from that one.), which, I think, would be more interesting while editing it.

Moreover, our teacher, also the director of this video, did not communicate properly with the students who appeared on camera. Most of them could not even remember the words to say clearly and sometimes made ambiguous statements, which greatly affected the results of this video.

Anyway, now my work is done, and it did bring me some sense of achievement. I wish that I could have a more interesting task next time.
