Got my PSP fixed!

I really wanted try Chains of Olympus once more after finishing the new God of War (it was awesome! ) on PS4. However, my PSP has been broken for years, it couldn’t be recharged via the charging port, back-light of the screen still worked, but no colors were shown. One of the speakers had a little noise, buttons were dirty, battery compartment couldn’t be closed properly as the joint was broken. In a word, it was a mess.

Luckily I could recharge it using the mini USB port and turn it on and I saw that the memory stick could be read which made me think that probably I could fix it. It took me several days to get the tools and parts to replace online and today I started to work on it.

Thank god that SONY did not use binder to assemble it, which made my job much easier than I expected. After unscrewing all the screws, I successfully removed the panel and I could get to all the parts that needed to be replaced. The screen was not very easy to be removed

And I made it! It was really touching for me to see this menu again after these years. Sadly I do not have any games to play on it now, which were thrown away a long time ago (Yes, including Chains of Olympus :( )), I think. Anyway, it is not hard to get the discs of games on line and they are really cheap after these years and I am glad that I can see this little thing on again.
